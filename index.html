About me

Hi, I'm Jenna Castillo, a full-stack web developer focused on building and designing exceptional digital products.
I spend my time building websites for small companies and designing personal projects. My skills include Ruby on Rails (7), PostgreSQL, HTML5, CSS(SCSS), Javascript(ES6), Stimulus(JS), GIT, and Github. However, I am constantly learning and am open to new technologies.

Before I was a developer, I worked in media production:

Some of my work and a me on a documentry set!

I enjoyed the technical aspects of my job, from dialing in exposer, to editing videos, to developing color codes. I found the science and technical aspects behind my work the most interesting. I was well established in my career, but I wanted more.

I wanted to create products that would provide more utility and value, but I was limited by my medium. When I discovered programming, everything clicked.

Now as a developer, I am focused on building useful, accessible, and dynamic web applications.
When I am not coding, I am often traveling, cooking, or bouldering!
